Import Members from a Drawing

Upload a scanned GA / erection drawing (PDF or image). The AI reads the member callouts (e.g. "TRUSS SHS75x75x3.0", "PURLIN CFLZ150*50*20*2.0") and drafts a member list you can review before adding it to the project. This reads labels on the drawing and makes a best-effort quantity estimate where members are visibly countable — it does not compute a certified take-off. Always verify quantities, lengths and weights against the drawing / MTO yourself before relying on them.

Gemini's free tier has no cost for typical use. Claude tends to read dense/cluttered drawings a bit more reliably.
